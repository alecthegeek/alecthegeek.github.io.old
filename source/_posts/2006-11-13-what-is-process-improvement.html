--- layout: post title: What is Process? tags: - Application Lifecycle Management status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- zx12bob asks what is process and who needs it. The answer to this question is easy -- the details and implementation are of course complex, but at the highest level it comes down to three things.
  • We must do things cheaper
  • We must do things faster
  • We must do things better
What does that mean?
Cheaper
  • Use Fewer people
  • Peform less rework (rework costs money)
Faster
Offer useful business services to users as quickly as possible
Better
Improve the user experience with better design and fewer bugs
The way to achieve this is to have everyone consistantly use the best process for the project. So now the complex questions:
  • What is the best process
  • How do we use it consistantly?
