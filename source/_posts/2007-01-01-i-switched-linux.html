Ubuntu is a complete Linux-based operating system
Well after the Novell/Microsoft debacle I switched from OpenSUSE to Ubuntu. It worked fairly well although wireless does not yet work. A big step forward is that my laptop now sleeps, and so should I!. Ubuntu seems to be the hot distro. at the moment, I recommend taking it for a spin if you are shopping for a new Linux. Some top tips:
  1. If the standard desktop ISO images do not work then there is an Alternative version. Try that
  2. Install and use Automatix
And a happy 2007 to all my readers as well.
